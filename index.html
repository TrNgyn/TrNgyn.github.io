FMCG Inventory Reporting

Inventory analytics tool built on ~1M FMCG transactions to classify SKUs by revenue contribution and demand consistency, supporting buying decisions across a retail portfolio.

  • Calculated tiered safety stock using service-level targets (95%/90%/85% for A/B/C tiers) and lead-time adjusted demand to balance stockout risk against holding costs
  • Built interactive Streamlit dashboard surfacing stockout risk alerts, overstock flags, and a daily reorder queue ranked by revenue impact
